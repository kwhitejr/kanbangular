About This Site

This is a custom kanban site that generates and tracks task cards. Cards can be given priority and assigned to system users, can toggle progress status, and can be freely edited. This site was built primarily with AngularJS and ExpressJS. The cards and users are saved and tracked in a PostgreSQL database and the Sequelize utility. The site is styled primarily with Foundation 6.
